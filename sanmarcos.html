Our Story

Taste of Home in Capitola

San Marcos Charquito is a beloved taco and torta spot nestled in Capitola, California — a small, vibrant spot that punches well above its weight. What sets it apart? Everything is made from scratch, starting with the tortillas pressed and cooked fresh every single day.

The menu is simple by design: tacos, tortas, burritos — done right. The kind of food that makes you feel like you're eating at someone's abuela's kitchen. The space is small, clean, and inviting, with a patio that's perfect for a sunny California lunch.

Regulars keep coming back for the al pastor, the carnitas, the legendary horchata, and most of all — the handmade tortillas. Located just around the corner from Gayle's Bakery, it's become a local institution with a 4.5-star rating and a deeply loyal following.
